About solvit africa

We are a Pan-African company tailored to produce the greatest tech workforce from Africa to the rest of the world using our unique model tailored towards creating employment through bridging the technological gaps between school curriculum and job market requirements.

Our Solution and Market
Positioning

Youth unemployment in Africa has been a troublesome agenda for quite some time. International job opportunities are available with a lack of means to get there. High unemployment rate especially for youth with tech background profile due to lack of exposure and required work experience in 1 specific tech skill. With a vision of 70% enrolment and graduation by 2063 by UNESCO. Currently only 32% graduate from tertiary education levels. This means that there are many graduates, (approximately 12 million) who have no jobs.

Our ideal positioning is forming strategic partnerships to expose Africa to the world positioning it as the hub of technological skill and workforce in a bid to create an ecosystem of tech experts and software developers to work for any company, both local and international.
